# RankFlow Product Context

## Register

product

## Product Purpose

RankFlow is an international SaaS operating system for SEO teams. It gives agencies, brands, and HODs the same level of operational control that Google Ads Manager gives PPC teams: account oversight, scan history, on-page intelligence, AI suggestions, task execution, reporting, and client visibility.

## Primary Users

- HOD / Super Admin: monitors all clients, risk, team workload, score movement, and reporting status.
- SEO Manager: owns client workspaces, prioritizes scan findings, assigns tasks, and publishes reports.
- SEO Specialist: completes on-page fixes, reviews AI suggestions, and attaches implementation evidence.
- SEO Analyst: reviews rankings, scan history, competitors, and performance data.
- Client Viewer: sees read-only progress, traffic, rankings, completed work, wins, and next-month focus.

## MVP Product Pillars

- HOD Command Centre: all-client MIS, summary strip, risk flags, and drill-down entry points.
- Scan Intelligence: versioned scan history, score deltas, severity counts, and comparison-ready data.
- On-Page Audit: page-level scoring across title, metadata, headings, content, technical, image, link, schema, speed, local, AEO, and GEO categories.
- AI Suggestions: source-backed recommendations with accept, edit, reject, resubmit, and implementation tracking.
- SEO Workbook: task CRUD, owner assignment, evidence, status, priority, and KPI attribution.
- Client Portal: read-only white-labeled progress view.

## Product Principles

- Action-first: every screen should surface the next operational action.
- Trust through evidence: every score, delta, and recommendation must show source and scan context.
- Dense but readable: this is a daily-work product, not a marketing site.
- International SaaS quality: polished typography, predictable navigation, responsive layouts, accessible colors, and clean empty/error/loading states.
- Expandable architecture: MVP mock data should use typed contracts that can later connect to Supabase, GSC, GA4, DataForSEO, Slack, and AI services.

## Anti-References

- Generic analytics dashboards with disconnected charts.
- Landing-page style SaaS mockups with oversized hero sections.
- Single-color blue dashboards without severity vocabulary.
- AI tools that hide source data or produce untraceable recommendations.
